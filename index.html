About Jenna

Hi, I’m Jenna Bernstein. I’m an intuitive card reader, astrologer, pendulum dowser, energy worker, certified yoga instructor, and meditation guide. I have been interested in metaphysics, perception, and spirituality for as long as I can remember. I first felt called to study astrology, then tarot, and now pendulum dowsing, along with a variety of healing modalities including restorative yoga, Jin Shin Jyutsu, and Reiki.
